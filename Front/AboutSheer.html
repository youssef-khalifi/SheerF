WHAT'S SHEER?


Sheer is the first classifieds app for donated objects.

Sheer is an application, and a website, that can be used 100% free of charge!

HOW DO I USE SHEER?


To post an ad for a food donation, simply click on "Add" . Then you can select "Food" and "Make a donation" and fill in the information.





TIPS AND TRICKS


  • Take Great Pictures

  • Write a Clear Description

TAKE GREAT PICTURES


Do not hesitate to take several photos of the object you wish to donate.

Take it from different angles, and most importantly, with a nice light to avoid it being blurry.





WRITE A CLEAR DESCRIPTION


The description of your item matters a lot. This is where you can give as much detail as possible about it. Feel free to indicate the color , size , and more, of the item you want to donate!
