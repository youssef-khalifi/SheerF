What is Sheer ?



Sheer is the first application for donations between individuals. Donate and collect unused items or food around you for free! Sheer is a useful, free and virtuous solution. Giving a second life to objects is an eco-responsible and anti-waste solution, promoting the circular economy.

Item donations



Sheer objet, brings together all donations of free objects around you and throughout Morocoo . Giving is easy with Sheer, post in seconds and offer a free book or any other object you no longer use to give it a second life .

Food Donations



Sheer food, brings together all free food donations throughout Morocco. Are you going on vacation and your fridge is full? And above all you don't like to throw food away. On Sheer it's easy, by posting an ad, you can donate fresh vegetables, baby milk and any food that hasn't expired. All together, let's donate food to fight against food waste .
